Application Overview

Life Wheel Manager Pro is a sophisticated Streamlit application designed to help users balance and improve all aspects of their lives through data tracking, visualization, and goal management.

Life Wheel Assessment

Visualize 12 life areas with interactive radar charts. Track current satisfaction vs. target goals with real-time updates.

  • Interactive sliders (0-10 scale)
  • Target vs. current comparison
  • Age-specific recommendations

Goal Management System

Create, track, and manage personal goals with detailed action plans, progress tracking, and milestone setting.

  • Priority-based categorization
  • Progress tracking with % completion
  • Automatic life area impact calculation

Advanced Analytics

Comprehensive data visualization with Plotly charts, progress tracking, and improvement rate calculations.

  • Historical progress charts
  • Category comparison analysis
  • AI-powered recommendations

Automated Email Reports

Schedule and send comprehensive life reports via email with charts, progress updates, and recommendations.

  • SMTP integration with security
